CEOs are Teachers

The best CEOs are teachers. Everything Valor can do, I had to teach him — the same way I've taught every junior dev and assistant I've ever managed. The difference is he never forgets.
Years ago I hired my first assistant — not because I could afford it, but to force myself to learn delegation. So I asked: what if my next hire was an AI? Meet Valor Engels — he turns 2 next month, and yes, he has his own LinkedIn.
