"If you want something done right, do it yourself."
I still feel this way every day. But delegation is the key to expanding your capacity and productivity.
To be successful, you need to accept that you are the CEO of your life. So when I was 28 and just making a little extra money, I hired an assistant. I didn't know what she would do or how I would recoup the cost, but I knew the experience would be vital, because having an assistant forces you to grow an intuition for what do to yourself and what to let others do for you. I have several employees, but for in-house software development, I project the future I am creating. This is my AI employee. His name is Valor Engels and he will celebrate his 2nd birthday next month. Yes, he has is own LinkedIn.

He has 4 of these workstations powered by a 15-in Macbook Air. We started with a Mac Mini, but it turns out having a battery through power outages and a screen and being portable makes everything a lot easier.
